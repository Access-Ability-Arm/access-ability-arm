Design notes: A) Classic D-pad layout. Familiar game-controller metaphor. Clear spatial mapping. All targets 100px. B) Maximum-size touch zones -- each direction takes up a full grid cell. Impossible to miss. Best for severe motor impairment. C) Camera-first with translucent edge zones for direction. Maintains spatial context. Compact bottom bar for height/grip. Most immersive but requires understanding edge-tap paradigm.
